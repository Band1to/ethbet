Design

The design of Ethbet is unlike that of any previously seen dicing project, allowing players to interface with one another instead of against a house.

The Smart Contracts Behind Ethbet

The Ethbet platform is composed of several different smart contracts. The first is a smart contract that allows for Ethbet Tokens (EBET) to function as a currency on top of the Ethereum network. There is also a crowdsale contract that allows users to exchange ETH for EBET during the crowdsale period.

After the crowdsale, development of the primary game contract will progress. This is the contract that allows users to place and call bets. In addition to this, a rewards contract will allow for EBET token holders to claim their ETH dividends, if applicable. Finally, a voting contract will allow token holders to vote on proposals related to Ethbet.

Contracts are developed in a modularized, testable, and secure manner. For more information about contract development, see the Project Architecture and Development Timeline sections of the Ethbet whitepaper.

Player Interaction With Ethbet

Player interaction with the Ethbet game contract is relatively simple, as there are few actions a player can perform and few variables that can be modified. When a player places a bet, they are able to set the following values: bet amount, house edge, player address (set to calling address). Future options may include bet type, and win probability.

After a bet is placed, it will be saved in the blockchain and will be available for other players to call. When a player calls a bet, all they must include is the bet ID that they are calling, as they only call a bet when they agree to the bet amount and house edge.

In addition to these two actions, a player can decide to cancel a bet that they have placed, which will give them a full refund of their bet amount, providing that no player has called the bet prior to cancellation.

Ethbet Tokens And Those That Hold Them

Ethbet tokens are not used to play the Ethbet dicing game, however are instead used to control it and fund it. Ethbet token holders are eligible to vote on proposals related to Ethbet, such as increasing fees or adding a new feature. Votes are weighted in proportion with the amount of tokens that a voter has, so someone holding 1,000 EBET has ten times the voting power of someone holding 100. Votes are then averaged with these weights to come to a final value.

In addition to having control over the direction of Ethbet, token holders receive 100% of the profits from Ethbet, paid in the form of dividends. This allows token holders to optimize Ethbet in the direction that they believe will be most profitable by voting and subsequently receiving the profits from the changes or features that they voted in.
