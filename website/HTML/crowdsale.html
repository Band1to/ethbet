Crowdsale

Ethbet token holders not only receive the full profits from Ethbet as dividends, but are also able to vote on proposals to improve Ethbet. Token holders can vote on values such as what Ethbet's cut of its optional player-defined house edge should be, allowing them to steer the platform in the direction that is most profitable.

The Ethbet Crowdsale is set to begin on September 10th 2017 8PM UTC, lasting for only four weeks. Don't miss out!

How to take part in the Ethbet Crowdsale

1. Use a wallet that supports tokens, such as MyEtherWallet, Metamask, Parity, or Mist.
2. Move Ethereum into your wallet. Ethereum is the only currency that is accepted in the Ethbet crowdsale. If you do not have Ethereum, you can buy it from websites such as Coinbase, or use a service such as Shapeshift to exchange other cryptocurrencies for Ethereum. Be sure to securely store and back up your private key.
3. Send the desired amount of ETH to the crowdsale wallet address below (not yet shown). Set your 'gas' to 200,000 for a smooth transaction. You will receive 1,000 EBET plus an early investor bonus per ETH that you send. The current investor bonus is displayed below.
Crowdsale not yet active
Do not send ETH from an exchange wallet such as Coinbase, Poloniex, and many others. These wallets do not support ERC20 tokens, including Ethbet. Your tokens will be lost if you use a wallet that does not support tokens.
Do not participate in the crowdsale if you have not read or do not understand the risks involved with platforms such as Ethereum and Ethbet.
Do not attempt to participate in the crowdsale if you are a resident of the United States. Residents in the United States are not allowed to participate in the Ethbet crowdsale due to the complex and costly requirements that the SEC would require.

Crowdsale Information

The Ethbet Crowdsale is scheduled to begin on September 10th, 2017, 8PM UTC. It will last for only four weeks after this period.
The ticker symbol for Ethbet is EBET. The total amount of possible tokens is set to 10,000,000. Of these tokens, 80% are available during the crowdsale (5% bounty, 5% partners, 10% Ethbet). The EBET allocated to Ethbet will be locked for 6 months by smart contract, ensuring it is in our best interest to make Ethbet succeed.
